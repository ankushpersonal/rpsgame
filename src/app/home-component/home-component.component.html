Rock Paper Scissors


Help!!

This is a simple game of rock,papers and scissors. In this game you have two modes. Single player and Multiplayer. In single player mode you will be playing with computer and in multi player mode you can play with your friend.

Controls: In single player mode
Left mouse click to choose the option

Controls: In Multi player mode
Keyboard keys: R is for Rock , P is for Paper and S is for Scissors. And your friend has Num pad where 1 is for Rock , 2 is for paper and 3 is for Scissors.
